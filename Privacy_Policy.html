This Privacy Policy is an integral part of newbody User Agreement. These Terms of Use (the «Terms») and the Privacy Policy apply to all visitors, users and others who access the Services. Your continued use of any part of newbody after the publication of the updated Privacy Policy will constitute your full ACCEPTANCE of any changes.

1. Privacy Policy changes

newbody reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy. This may happen from time to time. If newbody makes any significant changes, newbody will notify you by email (if you provide an email address), through the website, through the App or by submitting a new version of this Privacy Policy to you so that you accept it if newbody, for example, adds new processing actions or collects some additional Personal Data from you. All Personal Data collected by newbody will be processed according to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time of collection of this information. If the Privacy Policy changes significantly during this time, newbody will not use your Personal Data without your permission in a manner inconsistent with the Privacy Policy in effect at the time your information was collected.

2. Personal data about you that newbody collects

newbody collects the following Personal Data about you:

    General Personal Data
  • Contact information such as name, user ID, email address, mobile phone number.
  • Information about preferences that you provide, such as product or content interests, communication or marketing preferences.
  • Usage information about how you interact with newbody, such as your purchase history, content you viewed and areas of newbody you visited.
  • Device and usage information, which may include data related to your mobile device (for example, ID, brand, model, type, operating system, advertising ID and similar information); information about your use of features or notifications on the device; signal level related to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, temperature, battery level, and similar technical data; mobile operator.
  • Demographic information such as age and gender.
  • Your IP address and cookies when you visit the website.
  • Photos that you provide when using the App, using your camera or camera roll (if you have given newbody permission to access your camera or camera roll). newbody only gets those specific images that you choose to change using the App; newbody does not collect your photo albums, even if you grant access to them. newbody encrypts every photo you upload using the App. The encryption key is stored locally on your device. This means that the only device that can view the photo is the device from which the photo was uploaded using the app – the user's device. Note that while newbody does not require or request any metadata attached to the photos you upload, metadata (including, for example, geotags) can be associated with your photos by default. newbody takes steps to remove any metadata that may be associated with the photo you provided while using the App.

Personal information about your physical condition and well-being
Information about your fitness or well-being, such as your weight, height, and other details

newbody may use your information including your Personal Data and by accepting this Privacy Policy, you provide newbody with your explicit consent for the following purposes:
  • Analysis, operation, maintenance and improvement the App and marketing activities, and addendum new features and services to the App.
  • Evaluation your needs to identify suitable products or services.
  • Sending product updates or warranty information.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests and provide customer service.
  • Sending you push notifications, both internal and external.
  • Sending you marketing messages through various channels such as email, push messages, pop-ups, etc.
  • Improvement our App and marketing efforts.
Example: newbody uses your photos to refine and improve the quality of our Services. In no case newbody will use your photos and body features for any purpose other than improving its own product.
  • Conducting research and analysis, including for scientific and academic research purposes.
  • For any other purposes disclosed to you at the time of newbody's collection of Personal Data, or for any other purposes specified in this Privacy Policy. If newbody decides to change the processing purposes set out above, newbody will notify you of such changes before using your personal information for the new purposes and request your separate consent.
4. Storage of your Personal Data
newbody will store your Personal Data for as long as the App is installed on your mobile device or as needed to provide you with Services. If you delete the App from your phone or deactivate your subscription, newbody will delete all your Personal Data within 12 months and will only use copies of your Personal Data when it will be necessary to fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. newbody stores your Personal Data for a specified period of time so that you can restore all your information and settings if you want to return to the App. If you would like to cancel your account or request that newbody no longer use your information to provide you with services or would like to permanently delete all your information and Personal Data, please, contact support@appnewbody.com.
In some cases, newbody may then anonymize your Personal Data in such a way that it will never actually or potentially identify you. newbody may use such anonymized data for various purposes, such as training the Artificial Intelligence embedded in the App.
5. Security of your Data
The security of your Personal Data is very important to newbody. When you provide newbody with your Personal Data that is considered confidential or falls under the «special data categories» under applicable law, newbody encrypts the transmission of this information using certain technologies. newbody follows generally accepted standards for the protection of Personal Data provided to us, both during transmission and after receipt. Keep in mind that no transfer method or electronic storage method is 100% secure.
Therefore, newbody can’t guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about the security of the App, you can contact support@appnewbody.com.
6. Time to respond
newbody will provide information about the actions taken on your request related to your rights within three months from the date of receipt of the request for the longest period.

Correction of Personal Data.
You are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of your Personal Data that you submit to newbody. Inaccurate information will affect your experience using newbody's Services, as well as newbody's ability to contact you. If you think that your Personal Data is inaccurate, you have the right to contact newbody and ask newbody to correct such Personal Data by contacting support@appnewbody.com.
Access to your Personal Data and Data Portability.
newbody gives you the ability to access and update Personal Data in the App and your account settings.
Deleting of your Personal Data.
If you think that your Personal Data is no longer necessary in connection with the purposes for which it was collected or otherwise processed, or in cases where you have withdrawn your consent or object to the processing of your Personal Data, or in cases where the processing of your Personal Data otherwise does not comply with applicable law, you have the right to contact newbody and ask newbody to delete such Personal Data.
The right to object to the processing of your Personal Data.
In some cases, you may object to the processing of your Personal Data. Please be aware that the deletion of certain Personal Data inserted by you may affect your ability to use the Services.
8. Sharing your Personal Data and information
newbody will only share your Personal Data and information with third parties in the ways described in this Privacy Policy.
Common cases. newbody may disclose your Personal Data:
  • As required by law, for example, to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.
